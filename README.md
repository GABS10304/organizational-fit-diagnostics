# Organizational Fit Diagnostics

A data-driven framework to assess strategy, team, and culture fit.

## What this is
Organizational Fit Diagnostics helps you turn survey data and contextual inputs into a practical view of how well an organization is set up for the work it wants to do.

It is designed for teams and leaders who need a neutral way to assess:
- strategic alignment,
- team autonomy and collaboration,
- cultural readiness and learning behavior,
- gaps between leadership perception and team reality.

## Why it exists
Many organizations make decisions based on opinions, assumptions, or loud voices.
This framework gives you a structured way to compare what leaders think is happening with what teams actually experience.

## What it produces
The framework can produce:
- Strategy Score,
- Team Score,
- Culture Score,
- Largest Gap Area,
- a short AI-generated interpretation,
- and practical next-step recommendations.

## Typical inputs
- company size,
- market dynamics,
- transformation pressure,
- autonomy,
- clarity of roles,
- prioritization behavior,
- collaboration quality,
- learning culture,
- data usage in decisions.

## How to use it
1. Collect anonymous responses.
2. Score the answers across the three dimensions.
3. Identify the largest gap area.
4. Review the interpretation and decide on interventions.
5. Re-run the assessment after 30 to 90 days.

## Design principles
- Anonymous by default.
- Simple enough to use in one workshop.
- Clear enough to explain to non-specialists.
- Flexible enough to adapt to different company contexts.
